Samosa Trivia

Which country is credited with the origin of the samosa?

The samosa originated in the Middle East, specifically in Persia (modern-day Iran), where it was known as "sambosa." It was introduced to India by traders and became a popular snack in the Indian subcontinent, where it evolved into the spiced, vegetarian-filled version we enjoy today
